## Assumptions & Methodology

### Market Assumptions

1. **Market Growth:** 24.1% CAGR (custom AI model training)
2. **Market Penetration:** Conservative 0.1% → 0.5% → 1.0% (Years 1-3)
3. **Pricing:** Maintained at $29-$99/month with usage-based options
4. **Competition:** Moderate, with focus on differentiation

### Customer Assumptions

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Website Visitor → Signup: 5%
- Signup → Free User: 80%
- Free User → Paying Customer: 10% (Year 1), 12% (Year 2), 15% (Year 3)

**Customer Segments:**
- Pro ($29/month): 60% of paying customers
- Business ($99/month): 35% of paying customers
- Enterprise (custom): 5% of paying customers

**Churn Rates:**
- Pro: 8% monthly (50% annual)
- Business: 5% monthly (30% annual)
- Enterprise: 2% monthly (15% annual)

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Pro: $800 (27 months average)
- Business: $3,600 (36 months average)
- Enterprise: $24,000 (48 months average)

### Cost Assumptions

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Year 1: $50 (product-led growth focus)
- Year 2: $45 (optimization)
- Year 3: $40 (scale efficiencies)

**Infrastructure Costs:**
- Cloud infrastructure: 15% of revenue
- Scaling: Linear with usage growth

**Team Costs:**
- Year 1: $1.2M (14 people)
- Year 2: $2.4M (28 people)
- Year 3: $4.2M (42 people)
