## The Opportunity

**$2.1B Market by 2028** | **24.1% CAGR** | **Underserved Segment**

LoRA Trainer addresses a critical gap in the AI image generation market: training custom models is too expensive ($5-20/run), too complex (requires deep ML expertise), and too limited (no turbo model support).

- ✅ **Turbo Model Support:** Z-Image Turbo, FLUX.1, FLUX.2 (8-step generation)
- ✅ **80% Cost Savings:** $0.50-$1.20 per run vs. $5-20 competitors
- ✅ **Consumer Hardware:** Works on 24GB+ VRAM GPUs
- ✅ **Cloud-Native:** One-click training with Modal integration
