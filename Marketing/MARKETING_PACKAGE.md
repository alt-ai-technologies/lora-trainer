## Executive Summary

LoRA Trainer is a next-generation AI model fine-tuning platform that democratizes custom AI image generation model training. Built on cutting-edge technology including Z-Image Turbo, FLUX.1, and FLUX.2 support, we enable creators, businesses, and developers to train custom LoRA models on consumer-grade hardware and cloud infrastructure.

**Market Opportunity:** The AI image generation market is projected to reach $15.8B by 2028, with custom model training representing a $2.1B subset. Our platform addresses the critical gap between expensive enterprise solutions and complex open-source tools.

**Key Differentiators:**
- ✅ First-class support for turbo models (8-step generation)
- ✅ Cloud-native architecture with Modal integration
- ✅ Consumer hardware optimization (24GB+ VRAM)
- ✅ Training adapter technology for preserving model speed
- ✅ Web UI and CLI interfaces for all skill levels
