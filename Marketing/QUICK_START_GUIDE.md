## üÜò Common Questions

**Q: Can I use these materials as-is?**  
A: Yes, but you should customize contact information and add real data when available.

**Q: Are the financial projections realistic?**  
A: They're based on conservative assumptions. Adjust based on your actual data and market conditions.

**Q: Can I modify the pricing?**  
A: Yes, update pricing in all documents and the website to match your actual pricing strategy.

**Q: How do I create the pitch deck?**  
A: Use the `PITCH_DECK_OUTLINE.md` as a guide. Create slides in PowerPoint, Keynote, or Google Slides.

**Q: What if I need help?**  
A: Review the detailed documents for more information. Consider hiring a designer for pitch deck visuals.
