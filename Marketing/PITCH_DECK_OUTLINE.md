## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Colors:** Deep blue (#6366f1), Electric purple (#8b5cf6), Pink accent (#ec4899)
- **Typography:** Inter font family, clean and modern
- **Style:** Professional, tech-forward, accessible

### Slide Templates
- Use consistent header/footer
- Include slide numbers
- Maintain brand colors
- Use high-quality visuals
- Keep text minimal (6x6 rule: max 6 bullets, 6 words each)

### Key Visuals Needed
1. Market size infographic
2. Product screenshots/demo
3. Competitive matrix
4. Revenue growth charts
5. Unit economics dashboard
6. Team structure
7. Fund allocation chart
8. Milestone timeline
9. Market timing infographic

---

## Presentation Tips

### Timing
- **Total Time:** 10-12 minutes
- **Q&A:** 5-10 minutes
- **Per Slide:** ~45-60 seconds average

### Key Messages
1. **Massive Market:** $2.1B opportunity
2. **Working Product:** Production-ready today
3. **Strong Economics:** LTV:CAC >24:1
4. **Clear Path:** $150K MRR in 18 months
5. **First-Mover:** Turbo model training leadership

### Common Questions to Prepare For
1. Why now? (Market timing)
2. How do you compete with large tech companies?
3. What's your customer acquisition strategy?
4. How defensible is your technology?
5. What are the key risks?
6. When do you become profitable?
7. What's your exit strategy?

  - Technical architecture
  - Go-to-market details
